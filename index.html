About The Initiative

The Bhagavad Gita Classes initiative is a unique educational endeavor designed to introduce children and teenagers (ages 8–18) to the timeless wisdom of one of India's most revered spiritual texts, the Bhagavad Gita. Running successfully for over two and a half years, this program seeks to bridge the gap between ancient knowledge and modern-day living. At its core, the initiative aims to help young learners develop strong moral values, disciplined thinking, and a deeper understanding of their cultural and spiritual heritage. Through the study of the Bhagavad Gita, particularly the translation by Mahatma Gandhi, students are encouraged to reflect on life's fundamental questions, cultivate inner strength, and learn principles that remain relevant in today's rapidly changing world.
