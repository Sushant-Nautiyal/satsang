About The Initiative

The Bhagavad Gita Classes initiative is an educational endeavor designed to introduce children (age 8–18) to the timeless wisdom of the Bhagavad Gita.

At its core, this initiative seeks to help young learners develop:

  • disciplined and scientific thinking
  • moral values
  • better understanding of our religious, spiritual and historical heritage

Through the study of the Bhagavad Gita, students are encouraged to reflect on the importance of education (both ancient and current) in today's rapidly changing world.

The focus of the class is on the text of the Bhagavad Gita as translated by Mahatma Gandhi, the edition presented by Prime Minister Narendra Modi to the then US President Barack Obama.

5 Pillars of The Bhagavad Gita Class

These classes are built on five essential pillars that help students understand, reflect upon and apply the timeless wisdom of the Bhagavad Gita.

📖

Timeless Wisdom

Understanding the eternal teachings of the Bhagavad Gita and their relevance in today's rapidly changing world.

🌱

Self Discovery

Encouraging students to inquire, reflect and discover their inner strengths, values and higher purpose.

⚖️

Practical Application

Learning how the teachings of the Bhagavad Gita can guide daily decisions, relationships, studies and responsibilities.

❤️

Values & Character

Developing integrity, discipline, compassion, gratitude and ethical decision-making.

🤝

Community & Growth

Learning together in a positive environment that nurtures confidence, friendship and collective growth.

Key Focus Areas Covered in the Classes

The Bhagavad Gita classes connect timeless wisdom with history, education, contemporary developments and personal growth, helping students understand the relevance of the Gita in today's world.

01

Lord Krishna's Lessons to Arjuna

In the battlefield of Kurukshetra during the war of Mahabharata.

02

Gandhiji & the Bhagavad Gita

As a guidebook during India's war of Independence against the Britishers in the 1900s.

03

Studies in School

Understanding the importance of education and learning through the lens of the Bhagavad Gita.

04

Contemporary Developments

Scientific and human developments in India and around the world.

05

Discipline in Student Life

Academic excellence and extracurricular development through self-discipline.
